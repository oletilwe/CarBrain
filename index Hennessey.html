    • Hennessey Venom F5

    John Hennessey, that man from taxes created a company that modified already powerful cars to be more powerful. Yes john Hennessey is the founder of hennessy and the company is in taxes USA. The F5 name is a reference to the F5 tornado, the highest rating on the Fujita scale.

    The Venom F5 powered by a 7.4 L twin-turbocharged V8 engine. The engine in this car produces 1817hp and 1617Nm and it has a top speed of 484km/h or 301mp/h for my amarican audience. Along with that big engine it has a 7-speed single-clutch automated manual transmission with paddle-shifters and motivation from the rear. The roadster currently holds the record as the fastest roadster in the world.
