    • Ford Focus RS

    Most people overlook this one like the Renault firstly because its not a Golf GTI and secondly because its not from germany but they should be corrected because this is a really good car and powerful at that too. It is one of few that are as comfortable on a school run as they are on a durt road cause some people know what ford can do when it comes to rallying.

    The 2010 Focus RS is powered by a 2.5-liter turbocharged five-cylinder engine, producing around 300 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque and like the 2016 model it also has a 6 speed manual gearbox.

    The last generation Focus RS (white 4 door) is a 350 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque 2.3L turbocharged 4 cylinder rally car for the road. It is 4 wheel drive with a 6-speed manual gearbox that really engages the driver with short gears, all of this with the bazooka exhaust pipes that pop and bang really complete this car, thank god for no rev limiters here.
