    If there was ever a car that didn't need any introduction it would be this car, for those that dont know this car welcome to the mercedes c63 amg (w204). Amg's were thought to be europes muscle cars for a long time and this muscle cars heart is a 6.2l naturally asperated V8 which pushed out 451hp and 443lb torque from the standard engine with a 7-speed dual clutch gearbox.

    The facelift of the w204 (from 2011 - 2015) also saw an introduction of the 507 edition of the C63. It was powered by the same engine as the standard C63 but it pushed out 507hp as the name suggets and 610Nm of torque to the rear. There was the introduction of the w205 c63 and c63s amg a few years later. This new car used a smaller but powerful 4.0l Biturbo V8. The C63 had a power output of 496 hp and 650 Nm of torque and the c63s varient had 503 hp and 700Nm of torque
