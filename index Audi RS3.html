    • Audi RS3

    The Audi RS3 is part of the Audi A3 lineup but is enhanced with sportier features and more powerful engines. The powerful engine in question is its world famous 2.5L inline 5-cylinder engine that produces 394 if you are in europe and 401 hp in the states. It does the 0-62 mp/h in 3.6sec and with the dynamic pack option ticked it reaches a top speed of 180mph. These figures are for the latest RS3.

    Like its biggest rival the A45s the RS3 can now also drift with the help of its TORQUE SPLITTER. The torque splitter uses a separate clutch on each side of the rear driveshaft to allow it to shove 100 per cent of the available torque to whichever wheel the system desires basically drifting the car. No one ever thought of hearing the words drift and quattro in the same line before now.

    P.S. for the people that didnt know (and they are alot) the Quattro system isnt fully Audi's system. It a system called Haldex system and it is manufactured by a swedish company.
