    The most famous bakkie in South Africa and also one of the targetted cars in South Africa the toyota Hilux or as the streets call it the GD-6. This car is such a success that toyota decided to produce it in South Africa to make it easier to get hold of. The Hilux has many models, there is the Legend, Legend RS, Raider, Raider X and the GR.

    The range topping GR Sport GD-6 produces 165KW and 550nm of torque, with better suspension and a wider body thanks to the bigger tires.
