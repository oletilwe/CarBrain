    • This is the Dodge RAM TRX

    All amarican, loud and proud. This is the 702hp supercharged V8 beast from the amarican automotive company Dodge. It has 650 lb-ft of torque and is mated to a 8 speed automatic gearbox and is 4 wheel drive. The TRX is based on the standard dodge ram 1500, which is a already capable car offroad but the TRX brings its self to a higher level, often times being compared with a "baja truck" because of its capabilities.

    This car was made in response to fords VERY successful high performance "truck" the F150 RAPTOR which is also based on the standard ford f150, with a price tag of around R3.9 million in south africa i dont expext to ever see it on our roads.
