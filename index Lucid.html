    Lucid Motors is an American automotive company specializing in electric vehicles. Lucid Motors gained attention for its luxury electric sedan, the Lucid Air and this is the car we'll be talking about today. The Lucid Air is known for its impressive range, high-performance capabilities, and advanced technology features. The Lucid Air has 4 types from the Air pure, Air touring, grand-touring and the most powerful the Air sapphire.

    In a theoratical world the Lucid Air pure should be enough, it has 430hp and and from standstill to 60mp/h in 4.5 sec and this is more impressive because its RWD and weighs more than 2 tons. Luckily we dont live ina theoratical world and no amount of power is enough so we have the Touring which has 620hp and does the 0-60 in 3.4 sec. The grand-touring is where it really starts getting fun, the fun comes in the form of 819hp AWD 3 sec to 60mp/h and a limited top speed of 260km/h.

    Now this is where the fun truely is. The Lucid air sapphire the most powerful lucid in this lineup and more powerful than its closest rival the Tesla model s. The sapphire produces 1234hp from its electric motors and does the 0-60 in 1.9 sec, imagine from stand still to a 100km/h it takes less then 2 sec this is crazy to hear but it isnt about power and top speed here which is 205mph it has a range of 427 miles (687km/h) and in south Africa thats the same distance from Pretoria to just after Durban
