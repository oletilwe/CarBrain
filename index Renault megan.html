    The Renault Megane RS is a high-performance version of the Renault Megane. It typically comes equipped with a powerful turbocharged engine, sport-tuned suspension, and other performance-oriented features. The Renault F1 team developed this cars engine so the 1.8L turbocharged makes 296 HP and and 400Nm and has a limited top speed of 260km/h.

    The Renault Megane R.S. has suspension with hydraulic compression stops and they are added to each shock absorber to dampen the bump before they reach full compression. It also has torsen mechanical limited slip differential.

    The 2012 Renault Megane RS has a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine which had 265hp and 360 Nm with a a manual gearbox. When the 2012 Renault Megane RS came out it held the record for the fastest front-wheel drive car on the green hell.
